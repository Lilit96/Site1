Southern Ruckus Barbeque Sauce

Southern Ruckus gets along just fine with pork, beef, chicken, turkey and smoked sausage. It's a smooth savory blend of natural ingredients with mild spices, and a twang of vinegar. Southern Ruckus adds flavor to soups, stews, Cajun food and Texas round up vittles. Not recommended for ice cream.
